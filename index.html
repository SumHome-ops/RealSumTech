The Founder

Thomas
Eaves

Founder & CEO, Sum Technologies

Thomas Eaves has spent more than a decade in one of America's most competitive industries — real estate — learning something most technologists never encounter: what it actually takes to close a deal, earn trust, and solve problems under pressure.

As Lead Agent of Eaves Realty Group at Keller Williams and author of Millennials' Guide to Home Ownership, Thomas built his reputation as a puzzle-solver — someone who finds the angle others missed. He crossed over $100 million in career sales volume not by following playbooks, but by rewriting them.

Sum Technologies is the culmination of that same instinct applied to software. Thomas founded the company to build the tools he wished had existed — and to deliver them to the entrepreneurs who need leverage most.
